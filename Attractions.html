Where will your next adventure take you?

Lanzarote is an island that captivates visitors from all over the world with its natural beauty and exciting activities. Every corner is a testament to Mother Nature and human creativity in perfect harmony.

Timanfaya volcano

The Timanfaya volcano is the only active volcano in Lanzarote. Its volcanic activity continues today as the temperature ranges from 100 to 600°C at a depth of 13 meters resulting in extraordinary geysers of steam.

Rancho Texas Lanzarote Park

Rancho Texas Lanzarote Park is an animal and water park. It is a must-visit while you are in Lanzarote. With pickups from all resorts when you book, you can be assured of a full day out at the only Lanzarote zoo, with many shows like the Lanzarote dolphin show, bird of prey show, and the sea lions as well as the slides and waterpark. What are you waiting for?

Aquapark Costa Teguise

The family-friendly Aquapark Costa Teguise has something for everyone—big slides for thrill seekers, a child-friendly lagoon area, a jacuzzi for those who want to relax, and even an indoor paintball arena. Guarantee your entry on a day to suit your schedule by pre-booking admission in advance. Once at the park, the only restrictions are the opening hours, so take your time and choose how you spend the day.

Papagayo Catamaran

Lanzarote's Best Catamaran Excursion for Couples and Families. Sail onboard Ocean Lady with a maximum of 12 Guests and visit Papagayo Beaches. Use the Stand Up Paddleboards, Kayaks, masks, snorkels, fins and enjoy freshly made local Tapas and Drinks whilst you relax on anchor.

Submarine Safaris Lanzarote

If you want to explore the underwater world of Lanzarote without getting wet, then this is the tour for you. Descend 100 feet (30 meters) to the bottom of the Atlantic Ocean in a large submarine that can accommodate 44 seated passengers. Along the way, look out for marine life that you likely wouldn’t see otherwise—such as sea breams, eels, barracudas, and rays. If you're lucky, you might even see a shipwreck.

Private luxury Tours

Discover two of Lanzarote’s most striking landmarks: Jameos del Agua and Cueva de los Verdes. Enjoy a personalized sightseeing experience—on this private, half-day tour from Arrecife. Pick from a range of morning start times, meet your guide at your local hotel, and hop aboard your spacious, private vehicle. Head first to the Cueva de los Verdes, a lava cave that is both a natural attraction and a popular concert venue. Then, discover the Jameos del Agua, another cultural venue hosted within a lava tunnel.
